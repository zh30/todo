# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-16

This privacy policy applies to the Voice Todo app (hereby referred to as "Application") created by dev.zhanghe.todo (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

## Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include information such as:

-   Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)
-   The pages of our Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages
-   The time spent on the Application
-   The operating system you use on your mobile device

The Application does not gather precise information about the location of your mobile device.

### Microphone Access Strategy (Record Audio)

The Application requests the `RECORD_AUDIO` permission to enable the Voice-to-Todo feature. 
-   **Local Processing**: Voice data is primarily processed locally on your device for speech recognition and intent analysis using on-device AI models.
-   **No Storage**: We do not store, record, or upload your voice data to external servers for the purpose of training or analysis. The audio is processed in real-time to generate text and immediately discarded.

## Third Party Access
